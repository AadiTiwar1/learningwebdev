About Us

EnrichedCS is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free hackathons and workshops to middle and high school students. Our goal is to empower young students to learn how to code and develop a love for technology, so that they may pursue careers in tech and become the next generation of tech innovators.

Meet the Founders

EnrichedCS was founded by John Doe and Jane Doe, two passionate tech enthusiasts who wanted to make a difference in their community. With backgrounds in computer science and education, they saw a need for more opportunities for students to learn about coding and decided to start EnrichedCS to fill that gap.
